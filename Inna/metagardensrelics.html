Metagarden Relics

“Metagarden Relics” is teeming with animals, dead and alive, and largely devoid of people, as earthly paradises tend to be. It is also a liminal space, where one is not quite inside or outside, in paradise or in prison, at home or forever lost, leaping in ecstasy or locked in rigor mortis. Paradise is a place of ambiguity – populated by chimeras and a chimera itself.
